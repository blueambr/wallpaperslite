Summary

"Wallpapers lite" is a wallpaper browsing application that prioritizes user privacy. We do not collect personal data, source images through Wallhaven's API, and maintain transparent practices.

About Our Service

"Wallpapers lite" is a web application designed for browsing and downloading wallpapers. All images are sourced from Wallhaven through their official API.

Privacy and Data Collection

  • No personal information required: We do not collect email addresses, names, or require user accounts
  • No tracking mechanisms: We do not implement analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Local storage only: User preferences are stored locally in your browser
  • Third-party data handling: Image requests are subject to Wallhaven's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

Image Usage and Copyright

All wallpapers are provided by Wallhaven and are subject to the following terms:

  • Images remain the intellectual property of their original creators
  • Personal use is permitted (desktop backgrounds, personal devices)
  • Commercial use, redistribution, or claiming ownership is prohibited
  • For image removal requests, please contact Wallhaven directly as the content provider

Technical Information

  • Cookies: We do not use cookies, but we use Web Storage API to store user preferences
  • API integration: Search and browsing functions require communication with Wallhaven's servers
  • Service availability: We cannot guarantee continuous service availability due to external dependencies
